About

Welcome to the CSM Girls Who Code Club! We are a passionate group of students dedicated to promoting diversity and inclusion in the field of computer science. Our club provides a supportive environment where girls can explore coding, develop technical skills, and build meaningful connections with like-minded peers. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, there's a place for you here. Join us as we learn, grow, and empower each other to reach new heights in the world of technology!

What We Do

Coding Workshops

Join us for interactive coding workshops where you can learn new programming languages, techniques, and tools in a supportive environment.

Project Collaborations

Collaborate with fellow members on exciting coding projects and challenges, and unleash your creativity while building real-world solutions.

Guest Speakers

Gain valuable insights and inspiration from industry experts and guest speakers who share their experiences, tips, and career advice.

Community Engagement

Participate in community events, hackathons, and competitions to showcase your coding skills, make connections, and make a positive impact.

Mentorship Program

Get personalized guidance and support from experienced members through our mentorship program, and accelerate your learning journey in coding.

Social Events

Connect with like-minded peers, build friendships, and unwind at our social events and gatherings designed to foster a sense of community.
